Topic Overview

"Class, vertical irregularities are often more critical than horizontal ones because they can lead to story mechanisms and collapse. This exam covers the definitions in Table 208-9, specific percentage thresholds for Soft Stories and Weak Stories, and the special load combinations required when these exist. Watch out for the 'Extreme' definitions from ASCE 7-22."
